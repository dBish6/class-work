Welcome to Gary Blue's

It's like coming home.

Gary Blue has had a long and successful career. He spent several years traveling the world, meeting people and gaining a wealth of experiences. Following a brief stint as the Champion of the Kanto Pokemon League in 1998, Gary discovered his true passion - cooking. He loves bringing his amazing fried chicken and taters into the bellies of every customer that walks into his store, and filling them with his positive attitude with every bite along the way. Rest assured, every visit to Gary Blue's will leave you feeling like you're part of the family.

Where to find your next meal:

Gary can always be found at his diner, conveniently located at 2176 Lookcaster Dr, Fremont, right next to the largest shopping center in the city!
