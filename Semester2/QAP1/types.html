Types of Betta Fishes

Tail Types

One of the most defining way to classify your Betta fish is by the shape of their tails. There are many variations of the Betta's tail and all examples of these beautiful tails are listed below, as well as their color types are as listed. Some colours are rarer then others, Betta's range from all colours of the rainbow, so it's hard to list them all. Males and Females also have small differentiating looks, for example males generally have longer fins while females have shorter fins. There are hundreds of different Betta fish.

Colours

There are over 70 different wild variations, any kind of colour you can think of and of course some are more rare then others. Colours mixing, some Betta's have multiple colours, thus no fish is entirely the same. Some notable Betta fish colour group types are as listed below...

  • Albino Betta (White)
  • Black Betta
  • Blue Betta
  • Clear/Cellophane Betta
  • Chocolate Betta Fish
  • Green Betta
  • Mustard Betta
  • Pastel Betta
  • Orange Betta
  • Purple Betta
  • Red Betta
  • Yellow/Pineapple Betta
